Alpha Morgan Invest FAQs

What is Alpha Morgan Invest?

Alpha Morgan Invest is a retail online investment platform from Alpha Morgan Capital Managers Limited. This investment platform enables you invest N10,000 (minimum) from 90 days and above at a very competitive interest rate of 14% p.a. It is possible to also add N10,000 (minimum) to your investment at any point in time and a user can also decide to extend the tenor of investment for more than 90 days.

How do I start investing on Alpha Morgan Invest?

  • Create a free account by filling the KYC form
  • Invest immediately by funding your account.
  • Know your investment maturity date after funding your account (minimum of 90 days and above).
  • Provide the bank account details the matured investment will be paid into.
  • On maturity of the investment, the investor can decide to rollover or divest its investment

Bank charges or fees?

There are no bank charges for transferring money to your Alpha Morgan Invest account.

What is the amount I can start investing with?

You can invest a minimum of N10,000 and above, there is no cap to how much you can invest.

Making additional Investment?

Additional investment can be done at anytime but it must be in a minimum multiple of N10,000

How safe and secured is my investment with alpha morgan invest?

Alpha Morgan invest is a retail investment product from Alpha Morgan Capital Managers Limited registered and authorized as a Funds/Portfolio Managers by Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) Nigeria and professionally managed by Investment Bankers with over 52 years combined experience.

When can I divest my investment?

Divestment can be done on maturity of the investment i.e. after the agreed days the investment However, if you must divest before the maturity day, a penalty of 20% will be charged on the interest accrued till the day of divestment. The divested funds will only be credited to the bank account details provided by the account holder

How much can I divest from my account?

There is no cap to how much that can be divested.

How much can I have in my investment account at any point in time?

The minimum amount that must be in your account at any point in time is N10,000

Is there a Referral Program?

Yes! We have a referral program.

Each investment account has a link than can be shared to interested people who must also invest on the platform and a reward of N500 is given on each referral.

Note that if a referral divest before maturity, the amount credited will be deducted.
